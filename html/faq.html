Frequently Asked Questions

Learn about our top-rated customer service.

How do I purchase a travel plan?

We want to be sure that our customer experience is safe and secure, so our plans are purchased through human interaction. If you see a plan you like, please contact us and one of our travel specialists will assist you.

What sort of payment methods do you accept?

We accept payment through cash, check, credit and debit card, paypal, and venmo. We do not accept cryptocurrency at this time.

What happens if something goes wrong during a trip?

Plans change, Flights change, we get it! That's why we have a 24/7 customer service line. If something happens out of your control on a trip, give us a call and we will help you stay on track at no additional cost. Please note that factors in your control, for example. missing a flight, will be handled by us but may incur an additional cost.

Can I reschedule or refund a trip?

We work closely with our partners to set up deals for our customers. After a payment is processed, we send the payments directly to our partners, so they can not be refunded, however if a mistake is made we can likely get you a refund if you call us immediately.

Can the length of a trip be changed?

Again, we work closely with our partners to set up deals for our customers. Most of the time a trip's length can not be changed, however, customers are allowed to break out of a travel plan, for example, not taking your returning flight home and scheduling your own return flight later.
